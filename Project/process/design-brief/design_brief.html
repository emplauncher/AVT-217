Final Portfolio Design Brief

Brief

As a burgeoning young designer and artist, I want a portfolio website that advertises me and my skills to potential employers or clients. As I've learned from studying other artists' portfolio sites, good portfolio sites are not just pleasant to look at, they are easy to use and understand, display art well, and incorporate the artist's personality and style without sacrificing professionalism or intuitive design.

Objectives

I need to creat a portfolio site that properly represents me and makes people want to hire me, as well as showcase my work and development in my craft. I also need to make sure that the site displays my work professionally and is easy to navigate.

Visual Feel

I want my site to be professional, creative, clear, and stylish.

Audience

This site is for potential employers and clients to view my work.

Deliverables

A fully functioning portfolio website.
